What is Computer Science and engineerig

  • Computer Science Engineering (CSE) is an academic programme that
    integrates the field of Computer Engineering
    and Computer Science. It is one of the most sought-after
    courses amongst engineering students.

  • The topics covered in
    the course are computation, algorithms,
    programming languages, program design, computer software,
    computer hardware, and others.

  • Computer science engineers are involved
    in many aspects of computing,
    from the design of individual microprocessors, personal computers,
    and supercomputers to circuit designing and writing software that powers them.
